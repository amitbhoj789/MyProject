Current Challenges

“The greater the obstacle, the more glory in overcoming it.” – Molière

Partial list of Current Recruiting Industry Challenges

  • Too many mismatched jobseekers and jobs
  • Unverifiable and missing data in resumes
  • Stuffing keywords in resume to bypass the ATS
  • Misleading, duplicate or closed jobs posted
  • Ignored by some jobseekers and recruiters
  • Implicit bias in talent acquisition and hiring
  • ATS missing some qualified jobseekers
  • Outdated and unproductive talent solutions
  • Jobseekers submitting mass applications
  • Overwhelmed recruiters unable to respond
  • Wasted time, money and added risk & stress
  • Overall a poor experience for too many
