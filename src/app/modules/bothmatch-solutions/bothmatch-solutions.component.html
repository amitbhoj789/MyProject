Both MatchTM Solutions to address Current Challenges

By researching the root causes of talent challenges, Both MatchTM is introducing a new organic, open, collaborative, on-demand and identity protected talent framework.

Some key highlights:

Curate Match FrameworkTM

uses human insights and an artiﬁcial intelligence based DeepMatchrTM to deliver a Both Way MatchTM.

Both Way MatchTM

is created when a jobseeker’s curated skills, qualiﬁcations, and preferences and the employer’s posted or curated job details and requirements are organically matched.

DeepMatchrTM

system can save time, money and reduce stress by organically analyzing millions of jobs based on skills, industry and additional personal preferences. There are no guarantees, but a curated deep organic Both Way Match, significantly increases the chance of jobseekers being interviewed by potential employers.

ATS

are setup to look for speciﬁc resume keywords. As a result, some qualiﬁed jobseekers without the expected keywords are likely to be ignored. Both MatchTM helps jobseekers to use their skills and preferences and organically match with their ideal jobs.

Data Security

is a priority on the Both MatchTM portal. A matched jobseeker’s data will be shared with potential employers and jobreps via the portal only based on a jobseeker’s request.

Open Access

on Both MatchTM by default allows on-demand quick portal access to all jobseekers and professionals. Adding or editing data or subscribing will require additional validation.

Identity Protected Matching

when utmost conﬁdentiality is required allows jobseekers to look for a job without registration and in a private non-social environment.

Job Data Accuracy

is improved via the Curate Match FrameworkTM since it helps to reduce some misleading, duplicate, closed, and inactive jobs thus leading to saved time, money, and less stress.

Matching Bias

is avoided during the DeepMatchrTM process due to the exclusion of personal data such as name, profile image, race, ethnicity, gender, age, color, national origin, religion, marital status, disability, pregnancy, sexual orientation or ancestry. Jobseeker's skills, qualifications and curated information is matched with jobs.

Recruiting Efficiency

is increased since a curated Both Way MatchTM enhances the talent pipeline and allows recruiters to use the saved time to focus on upstream recruiting needs.

Ghosting

by candidates or employers is reduced because of the deeper level organic Both Way MatchTM results.
