Partial list of Current Recruiting Industry Challenges

  • Too many mismatched jobseekers and jobs
  • Unveriﬁable and missing data in resumes
  • Stuffing keywords in resume to bypass the ATS
  • Misleading, duplicate or closed jobs posted
  • Ignored by some jobseekers and recruiters
  • Implicit bias in talent acquisition and hiring
  • ATS setup missing some qualiﬁed jobseekers
  • Outdated and unproductive talent solutions
  • Jobseekers submitting mass applications
  • Overwhelmed recruiters unable to respond
  • Wasted time, money and added risk & stress
  • Overall a poor experience for too many

Both Match Solutions to address Current Challenges...

  • Curate Match Framework - Human and artificial intelligence
  • Both Way Match - Skills, qualifications organic match
  • Jobseekers - Save time & money and reduce stress
  • ATS - Reduce chance to be ignored without expected keywords
  • Data Security - Permission based data management
  • Open Access - On demand open portal access by default
  • Identity Protected Matching - Job search in confidence
  • Job Data Accuracy - Focus on real jobs and avoid misleading
  • Matching Bias - Avoid using biased machine learned data
  • Recruiting Efficiency - Saved time for other important tasks
  • Ghosting - Deeper match reduces chance for ghosting
